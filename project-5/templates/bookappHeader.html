How the Bookbin App works

Every once in a while we read a book that truly affects us, a book that might change the way we perceive the world around us. The bookbin app is for sharing these kinds of books with others!

Find a book:
Click on a bookbin that looks interesting to you and check out the recommended books!

Recommend a book:
Login with your Amazon account. Create a new bookbin and add your own book recommendations.
